Tortilla de patatas Recipe

Tortilla de patatas, also known as Spanish omelette or Spanish tortilla, is a classic and beloved dish in Spanish cuisine. It's a simple yet satisfying dish made with eggs, potatoes, onions, and olive oil. Here's a traditional recipe for making Tortilla de patatas:

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Optional: Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare Potatoes and Onions:

    Heat a generous amount of olive oil (enough to cover the bottom of the pan) in a large skillet over medium heat.

    Add the sliced potatoes and onions to the skillet. Cook them gently, turning occasionally, until the potatoes are tender but not browned. This can take about 10-15 minutes. The key is to cook them slowly to allow the potatoes to soften.

  2. Drain Excess Oil:

    Once the potatoes and onions are cooked, use a slotted spoon to transfer them to a large bowl, allowing excess oil to drain back into the pan.

  3. Beat the Eggs:

    In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt.

  4. Combine Eggs and Potatoes:

    Add the cooked potatoes and onions to the beaten eggs. Mix gently to combine, ensuring that the potatoes are well-coated with the egg mixture.

  5. Cook the Tortilla:

    Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour the egg and potato mixture into the skillet, spreading it evenly.

  6. Cooking Flip:

    Cook the tortilla for a few minutes, shaking the pan occasionally to prevent sticking. When the edges start to set, place a flat plate or lid over the skillet, and carefully flip the tortilla onto the plate.
